0 = ECHO - This unit produces an echo on the sound and fades out at the desired rate. (key 1)
1 = TREMOLO - This unit produces a tremolo / chopper effect on the sound. (key 2)
2 = FLANGE - This unit produces a flange effect on the sound. (key 3)
3 = DISTORTION - This unit distorts the sound. (key 4)
4 = LIMITER - This unit limits the sound to a certain level. (key 5)
5 = PITCHSHIFT - This unit bends the pitch of a sound without changing the speed of playback. (key 6)
6 = CHORUS - This unit produces a chorus effect on the sound. (key 7)
7 = SFXREVERB - This unit implements SFX reverb. (key 8)
8 = PAN - This unit pans the signal, possibly upmixing or downmixing as well. (key 9)
9 = FADER - This unit pans and scales the volume of a unit. (not currently used.)
